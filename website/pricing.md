## Why Enterprise?

The **Enterprise Version** is designed for organizations that require high availability, strict security compliance, and centralized management of agentic fleets across multiple departments or clients.

- **SSO & RBAC**: Integrate with Okta, Azure AD, or Google Workspace.
- **Audit Logs**: Complete traceability of every model interaction and tool call for compliance audits.
- **Dedicated Support**: Priority access to the Chain Nodes engineering team for custom integration support.
